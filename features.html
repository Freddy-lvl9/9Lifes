Features

What makes 9Lifes special?

Track Your Progress

Set goals, log your achievements, and stay consistent with daily or weekly challenges. The system keeps track automatically, so you can focus on getting things done.

Group-Based Gamification

Level up together. Users earn XP by completing challenges, maintaining streaks, and contributing to their group’s progress. Leaderboards rank members within each group, encouraging friendly competition and collaboration. Roles like creator and admin can assign tasks, reward effort, and shape the group’s vibe — making every team feel like its own little game.

Premium Access Made Simple

Users can upgrade to premium with a secure Stripe subscription. Just go into your dashboard and upgrade or cancel your subscription.
